## Project Overview

A custom-built, professional website to establish Deep Roots Drainage's online presence, generate leads, and position the company as North Dakota's go-to agricultural drain tile installer. The site will be fast, mobile-friendly, SEO-optimized, and easy for your team to update without any technical knowledge.

## What's Included

### Discovery & Strategy — $1,000
- Competitive research (other ND drainage companies)
- Content strategy and sitemap planning
- SEO keyword research for agricultural drainage
- Wireframe layouts for all pages

### Custom Design — $2,000
- Fully custom design (not a template) tailored to your brand
- Mobile-first, responsive across all devices
- 5 pages: Home, Services, About, Gallery, Contact
- Professional typography, color system, and visual hierarchy

### Website Development — $3,000
- Built on a modern, high-performance framework (Astro)
- Lightning-fast page loads (sub-1-second)
- Content Management System (Storyblok) so you can edit text, images, and pages yourself
- Contact/estimate request form with email notifications
- Mobile hamburger navigation
- Image optimization and lazy loading

### Content & Copywriting — $1,000
- Professional copywriting for all 5 pages
- Written for your audience (farmers, landowners, ag operations)
- SEO-optimized content targeting local search terms
- Clear calls-to-action throughout

### SEO & Local Search — $750
- Google Business Profile setup and optimization
- Local SEO schema markup (LocalBusiness, Service)
- Meta tags, Open Graph, and social sharing
- Google Analytics and Search Console setup
- Sitemap and robots.txt configuration
- Target keywords: "drain tile [city]", "agricultural drainage ND", etc.

### Launch & Handoff — $500
- Domain and hosting configuration
- SSL certificate (HTTPS)
- Cross-device and cross-browser testing
- CMS training session (how to edit your site)
- 30-day post-launch support for bug fixes
