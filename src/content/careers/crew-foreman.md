**Crew foreman** - North Dakota

**Job Type:** Full Time 

**Pay:** $32-$40/Hr.  Based on Experience

**Benefits:** 401k, Health, Dental, Vision, HSA, PTO, Paid Holidays, Potential Year‑End Bonus

**About the Job**

Deep Roots Drainage is a locally owned company specializing in high‑quality drain tile installation for farms and rural properties across North Dakota.  We are looking for a Crew Foremen to join our team. If you’ve got experience in ag drainage, enjoy leading a crew, and like seeing projects come together out in the field, this could be a great fit.

You’ll be running day‑to‑day operations on drainage jobs, ensuring customer expectations and schedules are met, problem solving, and making sure the work is done safely and correctly.

Responsibilities 

* Lead and work alongside a crew on Agriculture Drainage projects
* Coordinate with project manager to keep jobs on schedule 
* Make sure safety stands are followed
* Keep track of progress, materials, and project details
* Operate and Maintain equipment
* Troubleshoot issues and keep the job running smoothly
* Overnight travel required at times

Qualifications

* Experience in agricultural drainage construction preferred
* Comfortable leading a team and communicating clearly 
* Strong problem-solving skills
* Ability to interact with customers
* Familiarity with the equipment used in Drainage work
* Valid driver's license (CDL is a plus)
