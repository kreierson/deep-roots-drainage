**Heavy Hauler– Eastern North Dakota**

**Job Type:** Full Time 

**Pay:** $30-$36/Hr. Based on Experience  

**Benefits:** 401k, Health, Dental, Vision, HSA, Paid Holidays, PTO

**About the Job**

Deep Roots Drainage is a locally owned company specializing in high‑quality
drain tile installation for farms and rural properties across North
Dakota.  We are seeking a Heavy Hauler to
transport our drainage plows, excavators, and related equipment between job
sites. This role is essential to keeping our projects moving efficiently. The
ideal candidate is safety‑minded, experienced with heavy hauling, and
comfortable working in a rural, fast‑paced environment.

**Responsibilities**

* Safely haul and transport heavy equipment (drainage plows, excavators, support machinery) to and from job sites
* Secure loads properly and ensure compliance with DOT regulations
   
* Perform routine inspections of trucks, trailers, and tie‑downs
* Assist with loading and unloading equipment as needed 
* Maintain accurate logs, mileage records, and delivery documentation
* Communicate with the crew to coordinate equipment movement
* Follow all company safety procedures and state/federal hauling regulations

**Qualifications**

* Valid CDL
* Experience hauling heavy equipment
* Clean driving record and strong safety awareness
* Ability to work outdoors
* Strong communication and teamwork skills
* Familiarity with agricultural or construction equipment is a plus
