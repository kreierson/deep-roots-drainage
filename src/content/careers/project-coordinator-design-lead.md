**Project Coordinator-Design Lead**

**Job Type:** Full Time (Remote option available) 

**Pay:** Based on experience 

**Benefits:** 401k, Health, Dental, Vision, HSA, Paid Holidays, PTO

**About the Job**

Deep Roots Drainage is a locally owned company specializing in high‑quality drain tile installation for farms and
rural properties across North Dakota. We’re looking for a reliable Project Coordinator-Design Lead to join our team. As the Project Coordinator and Design Lead, you’ll turn landowners’ goals into well‑planned, efficient drainage projects. This role combines technical design work with project coordination, serving as the link between customers, field crews, and leadership. You’ll create accurate drainage layouts, prepare project details, and ensure each job is ready for smooth execution.

This position is ideal for someone who enjoys problem‑solving, working with mapping and design tools, and staying organized in a fast‑paced environment. Your work directly supports high‑quality installations and long‑term land stewardship.

**Responsibilities** 

* Develop accurate drainage designs, maps, and project
  layouts using GPS, Lidar survey data, and design software
* Review landowner goals, field conditions, and soil
  data to create efficient, effective drainage plans
* Prepare job packets, material lists, maps, and
  documentation for field crews
* Communicate with farmers, landowners, and vendors
  throughout the project lifecycle
* Maintain organized project files, digital records, and
  design archives
* Support operations with ones calls, and material
  ordering coordination
* Provide problem‑solving support when design or field
  challenges arise
* Contribute to continuous improvement of design
  standards, workflows, and project processes

**Qualifications**

* Experience in project coordination, construction,
  agriculture, or a related technical field Preferred
* Familiarity with mapping tools, GPS systems, survey
  data, or drainage design software (or willingness to learn)
* Strong analytical and problem‑solving skills,
  especially when working with field data and design requirements
* Excellent communication skills for working with
  customers, field crews, and vendors
* High attention to detail and accuracy in design work,
  documentation, and coordination
* Ability to manage multiple projects, deadlines, and
  shifting priorities
* Strong organizational skills with the ability to
  maintain clear records and project files
* Comfortable working both independently and as part of
  a team
* Proficiency with computers, spreadsheets, and digital
  project tools
