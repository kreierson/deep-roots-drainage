**Pump Technician- North Dakota**

**Job Type:** Full Time 

**Pay:** Based on experience 

**Benefits:** 401k, Health, Dental, Vision, HSA, Paid Holidays, PTO

**About the Job**

Deep Roots Drainage is a locally owned
company specializing in high‑quality drain tile installation for farms and
rural properties across North Dakota. We’re looking for a reliable Pump
Technician that will assist in the installation of lift and pump stations,
service lift stations, and diagnose non-working lift-stations, and all other related
pump and lift station related work. This role is ideal for someone who enjoys
physical work, problem‑solving, and working outdoors with a small, hardworking
crew.

**Responsibilities** 

* Assist with installation of lift stations and pumps
* Assemble and install piping, fittings, and pump systems 
* Load/unload materials and maintain organized job sites
* Follow safety procedures and company standards
* Support crew leads with general labor tasks as needed

**Qualifications**

* Experience in construction, plumbing, excavation, or related field preferred
* Ability to lift 50–75lbs and work outdoors in varying conditions
* Comfortable using hand tools, power tools, and small machinery
* Basic understanding of pumps, plumbing, or electrical systems is a plus
* Valid driver’s license 
* Strong work ethics, punctuality, and willingness to learn
* Ability to work as part of a small team and follow direction
