Deep Roots Drainage is a North Dakota drain tile company built by people who understand the land. We know what wet fields cost you — lost acres, late planting, and years of frustration. We're here to fix that.

## Our Approach

We don't believe in one-size-fits-all drainage. Every field is different — different soil, different topography, different challenges. We survey your land, study the data, and design a system that actually solves your specific water problems. Then we install it right the first time.

## Our Equipment

We run modern tile plows with RTK GPS grade control, allowing us to install tile at precise depths and grades even across complex terrain. Our equipment is sized to handle everything from small targeted runs to full-section pattern tile projects.
