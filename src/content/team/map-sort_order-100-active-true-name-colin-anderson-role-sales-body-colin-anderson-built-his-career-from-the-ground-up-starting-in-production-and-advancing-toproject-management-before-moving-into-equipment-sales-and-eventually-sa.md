Born in Grand Forks and a graduate of North Dakota State University with a degree in Construction Management, Colin has spent his career finding practical solutions for the construction and agricultural industries. He began his professional journey with three years as a project manager before transitioning into sales and management at an equipment dealership for six years. For nearly a decade, Colin has focused on sales and operations within the agricultural drain tile industry.

He enjoys taking on new challenges and helping producers solve complex water management issues. Outside of work, Colin is passionate about the outdoors and enjoys watching his two children participate in their various sports and activities alongside his wife, Mandee.
