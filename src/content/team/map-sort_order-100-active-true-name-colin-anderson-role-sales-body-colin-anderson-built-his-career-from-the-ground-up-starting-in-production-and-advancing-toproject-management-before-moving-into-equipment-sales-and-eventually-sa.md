Colin Anderson built his career from the ground up, starting in production and advancing to project management before moving into equipment sales and eventually sales leadership. He brings nearly 15 years of experience managing sales teams and bidding projects in the agricultural drainage industry. Prior to that, Colin managed sales for multiple farm implement dealerships. Born in Grand Forks, Colin was raised in Wahpeton, North Dakota. Colin holds a degree in Construction Management with a business minor from North Dakota State University. He and his wife Mandy have two children and a dog named Riggs, and they live near Fargo, North Dakota. You can reach Colin at canderson@deeprootsdrainage.com 701-499-3917.
