Corey Haag brings 17 years of experience in drainage design, surveying, and sales. He

covers the Central Valley through western North Dakota, managing key accounts across the

region. Originally from Garfield, Minnesota, Corey moved to the Fargo–Moorhead area to

pursue a degree in Construction Management from Minnesota State University Moorhead,

where he began his career in the drainage industry immediately after graduating. Corey and

his wife Lauren have two sons and two dogs, Brody and Boomer. In his free time, he enjoys

golf, fishing, and sports.
