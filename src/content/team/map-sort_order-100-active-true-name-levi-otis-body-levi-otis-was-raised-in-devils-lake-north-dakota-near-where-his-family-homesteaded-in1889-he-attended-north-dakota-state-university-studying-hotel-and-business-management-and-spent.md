Levi Otis is the President and Managing Partner of Deep Roots Drainage, where he leads business growth, strategic partnerships, and market development across the agricultural drainage industry. Raised in Devils Lake, North Dakota, near where his family homesteaded in 1889, Levi attended North Dakota State University and began his career managing hotels in Fargo before transitioning into agriculture and infrastructure development.

Levi has become a strong advocate for private property rights, agricultural drainage, water policy, and rural infrastructure. His work has included involvement with North Dakota drainage law, U.S. Fish and Wildlife easements, NRCS swamp-buster reform, and federal farm bill policy. He is passionate about supporting farmers, strengthening rural communities, and building long-term solutions through collaboration and innovation.

Levi and his family along with their two dogs, Boomer and Nakota, reside in rural Cass County. 

He can be reached at lotis@deeprootsdrainage.com or 701-499-3917.
