Levi Otis was raised in Devils Lake, North Dakota, near where his family homesteaded in1889. He attended North Dakota State University, studying hotel and business management,and spent eight years managing hotels in Fargo before transitioning into the agriculturaldrainage industry. Levi has since focused on advocacy for private property rights,agricultural drainage, water policy, and infrastructure development. His work has includedinvolvement with North Dakota drainage law, U.S. Fish and Wildlife easements, NRCSswampbuster reform, and federal farm bill policy. Levi lives in Argusville, North Dakota,with his fiancé Vanessa, their four children, and two dogs, Boomer and Nakota. He can bereached at lotis@deeprootsdrainage.com or 701-499-3917.
